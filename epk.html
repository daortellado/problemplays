Bio

Problem Plays is a three-piece band from Sacramento, CA, blending midwest emo charm with dynamic songwriting and powerful live performances. Formed in 2018, the band consists of Damian Ortellado (guitar/vocals), Sam Benavidez (drums), and Sam Fields (bass/vocals).

Since their formation, Problem Plays has evolved from their surfy punk rock roots to develop a distinctive sound characterized by vintage guitar tones, melodic vocals, and complex, thumping basslines, all anchored by Sam Benavidez's explosive drumming (also known for his work with international touring act SWMRS).

The band has established themselves as a fixture in the Sacramento scene, with performances at venues like Cafe Colonial, Old Ironsides, Golden Bear, and Secret Compound. They've expanded their reach to the Bay Area and beyond, playing notable venues including 924 Gilman, Oakland Secret, and The Ritz in San Jose. Their festival appearances include Jest Fest 2024.

Their discography includes the 2018 "St Helena EP," followed by a DIY full-length album "BEESWAX!" recorded during COVID and released in early 2024, preceded by a pair of singles in 2023.

New Release

Problem Plays is set to release their new album in Spring 2025, recorded at Rosa Mortem Studios in Sacramento and preceded by their single "Demon Future" dropping January 31st. The album explores themes of cultural identity and reconnection with roots, drawing from vocalist Damian's Paraguayan heritage, while diving deep into contemporary issues like parasocial relationships and mental health.

The record showcases the band's signature sound: intricate basslines, vintage guitar tones, and powerful vocal melodies, all driven by dynamic drum work that cuts through the mix. Lyrically, the album navigates personal struggles with anxiety, celebrates the support systems that keep us going, and contemplates the allure of starting fresh - all while maintaining the band's storytelling approach to songwriting.
